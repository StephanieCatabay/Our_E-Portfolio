INTRODUCTION

KAISArepresents a group of dedicated practice teachers deployed at Inerangan Elementary School, who work hand in hand to serve, teach, and learn with one goal—to make a positive difference in the lives of young learners.The group believes that teaching is not just about imparting knowledge but also about building connections, fostering teamwork, and growing together as future educators.Through cooperation, compassion, and commitment, KAISA stands as one—united in purpose, passion, and service to education. This e-portfolio is a compilation of the effort, commitment, and perseverance we have made throughout our student teaching experiences. It holds the memories of late nights, tight deadlines, and the lessons we learned along the way. In years to come, as we look back at this, may it remind us of our early steps in teaching and how far we've come. To the fellow student teachers Trisha, Stephanie, and Asher may we someday meet again, proud of what we've achieved and grateful for the memories we shared. This e-portfolio represents our aspirations and potential as future teachers in addition to serving as a log of our development. It displays the enthusiasm and tenacity we possess as we get closer to becoming certified professionals who will motivate the following generation. Let us be reminded by this voyage that each obstacle we conquer strengthens us and better equips us for the journey ahead.
