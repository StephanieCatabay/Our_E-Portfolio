Field Study 1

VISION

KAISA envisions a community of future educators who work together in unity and compassion to create a positive and meaningful learning environment for every learner at Inerangan Elementary School.

MISSION

KAISA commits to promote teamwork, dedication, and excellence among practice teachers by supporting one another in teaching, learning, and serving the school community. The group aims to inspire young learners through quality instruction, cooperation, and shared values.

PURPOSE

The purpose of KAISA is to strengthen the bond among practice teachers and to work hand in hand in achieving the goals of effective teaching, character development, and community involvement. The group seeks to uphold the spirit of unity—kaisa—in every activity and responsibility.

KAISA
(Ilocano word meaning “together” or “united”)
